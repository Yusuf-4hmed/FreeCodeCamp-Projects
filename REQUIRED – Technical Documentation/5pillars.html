5 Pillars of Islam

Shahadah

Shahadah is the Muslim declaration of faith.

The words of the Shahadah are repeated multiple times during the day.

Salah

Salah is prayer.

It is compulsory for Muslims to pray five times a day if they are eligible to do so, eg they have reached the age of puberty and are mentally and physically able.

    Here are the five prayers:
  • Fajr
  • Zuhr
  • Asr
  • Magrib
  • Isha
Zakaat

Zakah, or charitable giving,

encourages generosity and compassion.

Sawm

Sawm is the obligation to fast during Ramadan.

It teaches Muslims self-discipline, bringing them closer to Allah.

Hajj

Hajj is the pilgrimage to Makkah.

The trip is compulsory at least once in a lifetime for Muslims as long as they are fit and healthy and can afford to make the trip.
